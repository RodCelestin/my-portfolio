Rodolphe Célestin — Story

Reaching for the Sun

Outside design Show design story instead

Outside design, you'll find me on the tennis court, producing music for the music bands I play with, or går vilse i Sverige.

Music is a major part of my journey and my state of mind, I'm driven by it. I started music at a very young age. Over the years I came accros different instruments and music style: brit-pop, classic rock, industrial, depressive shoegaze, rock, black metal, nordic spirit...

But what stands out that I love is the pipe organ. I secretly nourished a dream to play it one day.

Playing in music bands was then logical (as a drummer), but it's a human adventure with challenges that taught me a lot over the years. I love producing "things" in general, and that's why I came across producing music for some of the band I've been playing with, from recording to mixing and releasing.

My inspirations: Novak Djokovic, Atticus Ross, Trent Reznor...

With nearly 10 years of experience across diverse design fields — from Art Director in agencies to Product Designer in start-ups,

My passion for craft has impacted many brands and products with design. For me, design is also about being adaptive, empathetic, and curious—embracing diverse perspectives to foster collaboration and achieve shared goals. I thrive in collaborative workflows with diverse expertises to deliver the best product possible.
